Purpose

This project is intended to make all aspects of life easily accessible to view on a single page saving time, any confusion, and make all of the required documents into a single list at the end with total cost. Currently trans folk have to disseminate information across multiple sites, be told varying degrees of (incorrect and correct) information, and many are unable to do so. By clearing up the process the project hopes to help trans folk feel assertive and clear up any fears or anxiety they may have. The information presented on this site is not legal advice nor should it be acted on as such. In addition information may not be current and is subject to change without notice.
